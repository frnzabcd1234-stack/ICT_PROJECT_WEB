Concept Paper

This project aims to explore ICT applications for social change. Through developing environmentally-friendly digital solutions, students will learn to design, innovate, and create meaningful ICT projects that have a positive impact on society.

The concept paper focuses on the integration of ICT tools to enhance awareness about environmental issues, promote sustainable practices, and empower communities through social innovation.
